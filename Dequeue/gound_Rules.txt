| ---------- | -------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| 1          | Can insert/delete from both ends | Acts like a combination of queue + stack    |
| 2          | O(1) insert/remove on both ends  | Much faster than `ArrayList` (no shifting)  |

| 4          | `ArrayDeque` is not thread-safe  | Use `ConcurrentLinkedDeque` for concurrency |
